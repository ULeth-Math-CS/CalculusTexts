{$f(x) =\sqrt{x-5}$ and $g(x) = f(x) = \sqrt{x-5}$}
{For   $f(x) = \sqrt{x-5}$ and $g(x) = f(x) = \sqrt{x-5}$

\begin{itemize}
\item $(f+g)(x) = 2\sqrt{x-5}$
      Domain: $[5,\infty)$
\item $(f-g)(x) =0$
       Domain: $[5,\infty)$
\item $(fg)(x) =x-5$
       Domain: $[5,\infty)$
\item $\left(\frac{f}{g}\right)(x) =1$
       Domain: $(5,\infty)$
\end{itemize}
