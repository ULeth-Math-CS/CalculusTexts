{$f(x) = x^2-4$, $g(x) = |x|$}
{For  $f(x) = x^2-4$ and $g(x) = |x|$ 

\begin{itemize}

\item  $(g \circ f)(x) = |x^2-4|$, domain: $(-\infty, \infty)$

\item  $(f \circ g)(x) =|x|^2-4 = x^2-4$, domain: $(-\infty, \infty)$

\item  $(f \circ f)(x) =x^4-8x^2+12$, domain: $(-\infty, \infty)$

\end{itemize}
