Domain: $(-\infty, \frac{5}{2}) \cup (\frac{5}{2}, \infty)$\\
Vertical asymptote: $x = \frac{5}{2}$\\
As $x \rightarrow \frac{5}{2}^{-}, f(x) \rightarrow \infty$\\
As $x \rightarrow \frac{5}{2}^{+}, f(x) \rightarrow -\infty$\\
No holes in the graph\\
