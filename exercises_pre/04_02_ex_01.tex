{$f(x) = \dfrac{4}{x + 2}$\\
Domain: $(-\infty, -2) \cup (-2, \infty)$\\
No $x$-intercepts\\
$y$-intercept: $(0, 2)$\\
Vertical asymptote: $x = -2$\\
As $x \rightarrow -2^{-}, \; f(x) \rightarrow -\infty$\\
As $x \rightarrow -2^{+}, \; f(x) \rightarrow \infty$\\
Horizontal asymptote: $y = 0$\\
As $x \rightarrow -\infty, \; f(x) \rightarrow 0^{-}$\\
As $x \rightarrow \infty, \; f(x) \rightarrow 0^{+}$
