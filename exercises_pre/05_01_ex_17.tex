{$f(x) = |x+1|$, $g(x) = \sqrt{x}$}
{For   $f(x) = |x+1|$ and $g(x) = \sqrt{x}$

\begin{itemize}

\item  $(g \circ f)(x) = \sqrt{|x+1|}$, domain: $(-\infty, \infty)$

\item  $(f \circ g)(x) = |\sqrt{x}+1| = \sqrt{x}+1$, domain: $[0,\infty)$

\item  $(f \circ f)(x) = ||x+1|+1| = |x+1|+1$, domain: $(-\infty, \infty)$

\end{itemize}}
