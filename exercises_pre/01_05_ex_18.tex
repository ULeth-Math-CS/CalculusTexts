{$f(x) =x-1$ and $g(x) = \dfrac{1}{x-1}$}
{For   $f(x) =x-1$ and $g(x) = \frac{1}{x-1}$

\begin{itemize}
\item $(f+g)(x) = \frac{x^2-2x+2}{x-1}$
      Domain: $(-\infty, 1) \cup (1, \infty)$
\item $(f-g)(x) = \frac{x^2-2x}{x-1}$
      Domain:  $(-\infty,1) \cup (1, \infty)$
\item $(fg)(x) = 1$
      Domain: $(-\infty,1) \cup (1, \infty)$
\item $\left(\frac{f}{g}\right)(x) =x^2-2x+1$ 
      Domain: $(-\infty,1) \cup (1, \infty)$
\end{itemize}
}
