{$f(x) = \dfrac{x}{3x - 6}$ \\ 
Domain: $(-\infty, 2) \cup (2, \infty)$\\
Vertical asymptote: $x = 2$\\
As $x \rightarrow 2^{-}, f(x) \rightarrow -\infty$\\
As $x \rightarrow 2^{+}, f(x) \rightarrow \infty$\\
No holes in the graph\\
Horizontal asymptote: $y = \frac{1}{3}$ \\
As $x \rightarrow -\infty, f(x) \rightarrow \frac{1}{3}^{-}$\\
As $x \rightarrow \infty, f(x) \rightarrow \frac{1}{3}^{+}$}
