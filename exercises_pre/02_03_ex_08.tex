$y$-intercept $(0, 4)$\\
Domain: $(-\infty, \infty)$ \\
Range: $\left(-\infty,  \frac{73}{12} \right]$ \\
Increasing on $\left(-\infty, \frac{5}{6}\right]$ \\
Decreasing on $\left[ \frac{5}{6}, \infty\right)$ \\
Vertex $\left(\frac{5}{6}, \frac{73}{12} \right)$ is a maximum \\
Axis of symmetry $x = \frac{5}{6}$
