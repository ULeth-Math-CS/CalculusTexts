No $x$-intercepts\\
No $y$-intercepts\\
Vertical asymptote: $x = 0$\\
As $x \rightarrow 0^{-}, \; f(x) \rightarrow \infty$\\
As $x \rightarrow 0^{+}, \; f(x) \rightarrow \infty$\\
Horizontal asymptote: $y = 0$\\
As $x \rightarrow -\infty, \; f(x) \rightarrow 0^{+}$\\
As $x \rightarrow \infty, \; f(x) \rightarrow 0^{+}$
